Installation Guide

How to Install the APK

Direct APK installation means no Google Play account is needed. Follow these simple steps.

1

Enable "Install Unknown Apps"

On your Android phone, go to Settings → Security (or Privacy on newer phones) and enable Install Unknown Apps for your browser or file manager. This allows APK files downloaded from outside the Play Store to be installed.

2

Download the APK

Tap the download button on this page. The APK file (campaignmasta-v1.apk) will download to your phone's Downloads folder. Make sure you have at least 50MB of free storage space.

3

Open and Install

Open your Files or Downloads app and tap on the APK file. Tap Install when prompted. The installation takes about 30 seconds.

4

Log In with Your Account

Open CampaignMasta and log in with the username and password provided by your campaign manager. Your data syncs automatically from the web platform.
