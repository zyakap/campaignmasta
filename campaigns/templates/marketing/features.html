Supporter CRM

Your most important asset is knowing who supports you and where they live. CampaignMasta lets you build a detailed, searchable database of every voter — organised by province, district, LLG, ward, and village.

  • Track support level: Strong, Leaning, Undecided, Opposed
  • Record gender, occupation, clan, and household details
  • Filter and search by ward, village, or any field
  • See coverage heat-map — where are your gaps?
  • Link supporters to polling locations
  • Tag VIP supporters for special attention
