Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the K3,000 setup fee for?

The setup fee covers configuring your electorate's geographic data (wards, LLGs, districts), importing your existing supporter data, training your campaign manager, and 30 days of onboarding support. It is paid once only.

How is "fortnight" billing handled?

Your subscription renews every two weeks (14 days). You can pay by bank transfer or mobile money. We'll send a reminder before each billing date.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. There are no penalties for changing plans.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — new accounts start on a free trial so you can explore the platform before committing. Contact us to activate your trial.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export all your data at any time. We retain your data for 90 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.

Does the Android app cost extra?

No. The Android app is included in all subscription plans at no additional cost. You can install it on as many team devices as you need.

Can I use CampaignMasta for a Provincial seat?

Yes. CampaignMasta supports both District Open and Regional/Provincial seats. The geographic structure adjusts automatically based on your electorate type.

How do I get started?

Contact us via email or WhatsApp to start the setup process. We'll configure your electorate, create your account, and get your team trained within a few days.
